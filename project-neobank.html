CHALLENGE

Simplifying complex financial data for digital natives

NeoBank, a fintech startup, approached us with a common problem in the digital banking space: their existing dashboard was overwhelming for users. With multiple charts, complex metrics, and no clear hierarchy, users struggled to understand their financial health at a glance.

The original interface showed 15+ data points simultaneously, causing cognitive overload. User testing revealed that 68% of users couldn't quickly find their current balance, and 45% abandoned the dashboard within 30 seconds.

"

Users shouldn't need a finance degree to understand their own money.

‚Äî Product Manager, NeoBank
SOLUTION

Intuitive hierarchy with progressive disclosure

We redesigned the dashboard with a mobile-first approach, focusing on three key principles:

01

Glanceable Overview

Critical information (balance, recent transactions, upcoming bills) visible without scrolling. Color-coded indicators for positive/negative changes.

02

Progressive Disclosure

Detailed analytics hidden behind interactive elements. Users can drill down into spending categories, investment performance, or savings goals as needed.

03

Personalized Insights

AI-powered insights surface relevant information based on user behavior. "You're spending 20% more on dining this month" instead of raw data.
